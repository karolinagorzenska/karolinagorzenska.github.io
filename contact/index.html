I am a Gdynia-based Polish artist born in 1997, currently studying at Minerva Art Academy in Groningen, the Netherlands. My main field of work is film photography. I research intimacy in relation to natural world. I use my creative drive to gather insights about mostly myself, my inner world, but also the outer world, through my relationship with it. I do not think there is any gap or border between species, between man and the rest of the realm of life. I try to dwell in this unity and make it visible through my photography. It is my belief we build our reality ourselves by dreaming it up, and this is my mission as an artist to share my dreams and help create a better reality.
